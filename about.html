About Us

Founded in 2008 and headquartered in China's manufacturing heartland, WaterbearIntl is a professional industrial products export company. We are dedicated to delivering high-quality Chinese-manufactured industrial products to customers worldwide.

With over 5,000 sqm of warehouse facilities and strategic partnerships with 200+ premium suppliers, our annual exports exceed $200 million. Our team consists of experienced international trade specialists, technical engineers, and multilingual support staff.
